Library Services

"Equip yourself with foundational writing skills for college success. Workshop recordings, tip sheets and practice activities now available"(RRC Polytech.2025).

RRC Polytech's Library Services offer access to many arrays of academic resources, including books, journals, online databases, and research materials. Students can also benefit from workshops, citation assistance and one-on-one research support to enhance their academic work and research projects.

What do you gain from services?

  • Research Support: Students learn how to access, evaluate and organize information for academic papers and projects.
  • Citation Assistance: Get help with proper citation formats such as APA, MLA and Chicago style to avoid plagiarism.
  • Academic Success Workshops: Participate in workshops that teach effective study techniques and improve academic writing skills.

How to access these services?

  • Visiting the Library website: RRC Library Services
  • Scheduling one-on-one research with a librarian for personalized assistance.
  • Participating in library-hosted workshops to improve research skills and study techniques.

APA References

APA is the style of documentation of sources used by the American Psychological Association. This form of writing research papers is used mainly in the social sciences, like psychology, anthropology, sociology, as well as education and other fields.
