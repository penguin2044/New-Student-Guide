About

Starting college can be both an exciting and challenging experience. Red River College Polytechnic (RRC Polytech) offers various services to help students navigate academic and personal obstacles. This guide focuses on two essential services: Counselling Services and Library Services. Each service is designed to enhance your well-being and academic success, while being aligned with communication strategies that will help you thrive during your studies.

Student services

International Student Services

International Student Services offers support and resources to assist international students in adapting to life in Canada and succeeding in their studies.

Counselling Services

Counselling Services at RRC Polytech are designed to help students manage personal, emotional, and academic challenges during their time at RRC Polytech.

Library Services

RRC Polytech's Library Services offer access to many arrays of academic resources, including books, journals, online databases, and research materials.

Student Employment Services

provides the tools and support needed to enter the job market successfully. The service also provides opportunities for students to build professional relationships that will support their long-term career growth.

Academic Success Services

The Academic Success Centre at RRC Polytech provides a wide range of free academic supports designed to help students succeed in their studies.

Accessibility Services

supporting students with documented disabilities by ensuring fair and equal access to academic programs, services, and campus facilities.
