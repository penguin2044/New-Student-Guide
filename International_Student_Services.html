International Student Services

International Student Services offers support and resources to assist international students in adapting to life in Canada and succeeding in their studies. This service is incredibly valuable as it helps international students transition smoothly into a new academic and cultural environment. By providing guidance on essential topics such as study permits, work opportunities, and adjusting to Canadian education, students can confidently focus on their academic and career goals. International Student Services at RRC Polytech, promoting “Study, live and work in the heart of Canada” (RRC Polytech, 2025).

  • Visa and Immigration Support: Assists students in understanding study permits, work permits, and post-graduate work options.
  • Orientation Programs: Deliver vital information regarding Canadian culture, RRC Polytech policies, and academic expectations.
  • Cultural Integration and Mentorship: Provides mentorship opportunities and social activities to help international students connect with their peers.
  • Health and Wellness Support: Offers guidance on health insurance, mental health services, and well-being resources.
  • Employment Support: Informs students about work permits, job search strategies, and workplace expectations in Canada.
  • Further information: https://www.rrc.ca/international/

What do you gain from services?

The service helps students learn to make decisions regarding their studies and careers while developing skills to organize their academic and personal lives. By participating in cultural activities and workshops, students enhance communication skills and build confidence in engaging with peers. The service promotes planning for academic success, critical questioning, personal reflection, and addressing challenges during their transition.

How to access these services?

  • Visit the International Student Services
  • website: https://www.rrc.ca/international/
  • Email ISS for support: international@rrc.ca
  • Attend international student orientation events and mentorship programs.
  • Speak with an International Student Advisor for academic and personal guidance.
