Academic Success Centre

The Academic Success Centre at RRC Polytech provides a wide range of free academic supports designed to help students succeed in their studies. Whether you're trying to better understand new course material, preparing for exams, or improving your study habits, the Academic Success Centre offers personalized support tailored to your learning goals. In addition, services promote intercultural competence and inclusive learning experiences that empower all students to thrive academically.

The Academic Success Centre focuses on the following support services (RRC Polytech, 2025):

  • Tutoring & Academic Coaching: One-on-one or small group support to help students grasp course content, develop critical thinking, and stay on track with their studies.
  • Study Groups & Review Workshops: Collaborative learning sessions designed to reinforce course material and help students prepare for quizzes, tests, and final exams.
  • Study Skills & Diversity Workshops: Workshops focused on time management, note-taking, test-taking strategies, and intercultural awareness.
  • Health and Wellness Support: Offers guidance on health insurance, mental health services, and well-being resources.
  • EAL (English as an Additional Language) Supports: Language development support including academic writing, reading, listening, and speaking for students whose first language is not English.
  • Further information: https://www.rrc.ca/academicsuccess/

What do you gain from services?

The Academic Success Centre helps students become independent and confident learners. By participating in tutoring sessions and workshops, students develop effective study strategies, enhance time management, and strengthen their understanding of academic content. Services also help improve communication and collaboration skills, especially in intercultural contexts. These supports contribute to both immediate academic success and long-term personal growth.

How to access these services?

  • Visiting the Academic Success Centre website: https://www.rrc.ca/academicsuccess/
  • Booking appointments with tutors or academic coaches online or in person.
  • Attend international student orientation events and mentorship programs.
  • Joining study groups, review sessions, or academic skills workshops offered throughout the term.
  • Participating in EAL support sessions tailored to language learning and academic success.
