Counselling Services

“Welcome to student Counselling Services! We provide free, confidential counselling services to all RRC Polytech students. We are here to help students work through personal struggles, develop skills to manage better, and enjoy their college experience” (RRC Polytech, 2025).

Counselling Services at RRC Polytech are designed to help students manage personal, emotional, and academic challenges. Whether you're dealing with stress, anxiety, relationship issues, or academic pressure, the service offers personalized one-on-one sessions, workshops, and resources to support mental health and personal growth.

What do you gain from services?

Counseling Services at RRC Polytech utilizes several key communication strategies to support student's mental well-being. Active listening creates a safe environment where students can express their concerns without judgment, while empathy helps counselors understand the student's emotional states, fostering a compassionate and very supportive atmosphere. Reading body language and facial expressions strengthens support and trust between counselors and students. Through clarity and conciseness, counselors provide clear guidance on mental health resources and coping strategies, ensuring students understand the steps needed to improve their well-being. Feedback plays a crucial role as counselors offer constructive input on how students navigate personal challenges, build strength and manage interpersonal issues. Finally, interpersonal communication is at the core of counselling, as one-on-one sessions provide a platform for open and meaningful dialogue between students and counselors, reinforcing trust and understanding.

How to access these services?

  • Visiting the counselling Services website: https://www.rrc.ca/counselling/
  • Booking an appointment with a counselor through the online portal or at the campus office.
  • Participating in mental health workshops and group sessions offered throughout the year.
